Tri2Vec Clinical Trial Matching

Call our Twilio number and tell us about your medical situation or the type of trial you want to join.

Your description is converted into a vector embedding and compared daily to new and ongoing trials.

If we find a match we will send you an SMS with a link to the trial so you can get in touch with the researchers.

We keep track of which trials we have already sent you so you won't receive the same notification twice.
